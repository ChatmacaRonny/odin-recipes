Carbonara

Pasta based meal with pork and cheese.

Easy to make and delicious

>

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Joue de porc
  • Carbonara cheese
  • Pepper
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Render de pork fat in the cast iron
  3. Grill until crunchy
  4. whisk eggs and cheese in bowl
  5. Lower the pan temparature
  6. Add the pasta into the pan with the grilled pork
  7. Add pasta water and the eggs mix and whisk vigorously
  8. Remove the pan from the head is needed
  9. Garnish with cheese
  10. FINISHED!
